    I am currently a third year PhD at University of California, Berkeley majoring in Transportation Engineering. I am working with Prof. Raja Sengupta and Prof. James Demmel. Previously, I received my Master degree in Transportation Engineering from University of California, Berkeley advised by Prof. Mark Hansen, Prof. Susan Shaheen, Prof. Joan Walker, Prof. Alexander Skabardonis.

    My research interests are parallel computation/ traffic operation simulation/ urban air mobility. Currently, my research mainly focuses on accelerating the convergence/simulation speed of metropolitan scale transportation systems using novel system schemes and machine learning methods. I will graduate from UCB in December 2024 and intend to apply for faculty position. Below are the strengths of mine:

  • Deep Learning based traffic congestion prediction.
  • Urban Air Mobility traffic management, conflict detection and avoidance.
  • GPU based parallel traffic operation simulation and data acquisition.
  • Reinforcement Learning based multi-line bus bunching problem.
